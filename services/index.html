Structured Finance

Travelers Capital delivers structured non-recourse project finance solutions for projects to meet the specific needs of project sponsors. Transaction size range from as low as $5 million to over $100 million. With our experienced and dedicated team of structured finance experts, specialized in the alternative energy sector, we deliver financing solutions in a timely and cost effective manner at competitive market rates.

Our structured financing solutions rely on the cash flows from the underlying project to service the debt. This type of financing provides an “off balance sheet” solution and does not typically rely on guarantees from shareholders.

We provide the following solutions:

  • Construction & term financing with terms up to 40 years matching the maturity of the offtake agreement.
  • Term debt for refinancing or acquisition of operating projects
  • Holdco loans to back-leverage sponsor equity to enhance project IRR or redeploy capital
  • Subordinated/mezzanine debt to reduce the amount of equity required to invest in projects and enhance project returns
  • Project equity via strategic partnerships with Travelers Capital partners

Administration Services

Our involvement in a project's financing continues long after a transaction closes. As loan agent and security agent, we provide administrative services for our clients acting as the single point of contact between the lenders and borrowers resulting in a more streamlined and efficient process.

Working on behalf of our lenders, Travelers Capital key responsibilities acting as loan and security agent include the following:

  • Centralized loan payment processing
  • Construction monitoring and coordination of construction draws
  • Financial reporting and annual budget reviews
  • Covenant compliance reporting
  • General loan administration
  • Handling all waivers and amendments
  • Attending to all security and insurance requirements
  • Secure storage of loan security documents
