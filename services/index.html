Expertise to create tailored solutions for you

We offer a broad set of integrated solutions to our clients, from arranging private debt and equity to advising on financing options, strategic acquisitions and divestitures and partnership opportunities.

In arranging debt financing, we bridge the gap between borrowers/sponsors and our lender partners, which include institutional investors, life insurance companies and non-bank lenders. We are lender and technology agnostic – our job is to procure the most attractive terms and structure for our clients – and have solutions for deals ranging from $5 million to $200+ million.

As financial advisor, we provide thorough, objective advice and support for clients engaged in capital raising, buy-side and sell-side transactions and other strategic initiatives, specializing in the alternative energy and infrastructure sectors.

Structured Finance

Travelers Capital delivers structured and project finance solutions to meet the specific needs of our project sponsor clients. We specialize in mid-market transactions with deals ranging from $5 million to $200+ million. With our dedicated team of structured finance experts, we deliver customized solutions in a timely and cost-effective manner.

Structured finance solutions we provide include:

  • Short-term and long-term construction plus term project financing
  • Term loans for refinancing or acquisition of operating projects/completed infrastructure
  • Holdco/subordinated loans to back-leverage sponsor equity
  • Mezzanine and bridge financing
  • Equipment and programmatic financing
  • Project equity/development capital via strategic partnerships with Travelers Capital clients

Corporate Finance

Travelers Capital can arrange bespoke corporate finance solutions for companies across a wide range of industries seeking growth capital. We are not constrained by any governance mandate that limits the types of solutions we can provide – our job is to facilitate the highest-value solution for our clients given their specific needs.

Corporate finance solutions we provide include:

  • Corporate term and revolving loans
  • Construction and bridge financing
  • Working capital facilities
  • Asset-based lending
  • Subordinated and mezzanine financing
  • Uni-tranche financing
  • Monetization of royalty streams
  • Surety bond products to replace cash funded reserves or other credit support instruments
  • Equity growth capital via strategic partnerships with Travelers Capital clients

Financial Advisory

Travelers Capital provides financial advisory services and transaction support for clients engaged on a wide array of transactions, specializing in project financing and asset divestitures or acquisitions. We have the expertise necessary to provide cradle-to-grave transaction execution and structure our mandates to align our interests with those of our clients.

Financial advisory services we provide include:

  • Procurement of structured or corporate debt
  • Sale or purchase of assets or companies (including competitive processes/auctions)
  • Request-for-Proposal advisory (conducting or responding)
  • Reviewing bankability of projects/infrastructure and material agreements
  • Financial modelling and sensitivity analysis
  • Transaction structuring and due diligence support
  • Identification of capital sources (equity and debt)
  • Preparation of transaction process materials (information memoranda, process letters, teasers, etc.)

Administration Services

Travelers Capital provides the full suite of loan and/or security agent services on behalf of many of our lender partners. We serve as the single point of contact between lenders and borrowers, resulting in a more efficient loan administration process and reducing the workload for both sides. We can also provide asset administration/special purpose vehicle management services for sponsors who can benefit from third party administrative support.

Administration services we provide include:

  • Centralized drawdown and loan payment processing
  • Construction monitoring and reporting
  • Financial reporting and annual budget/plan reviews
  • Covenant compliance
  • Management of waivers, consents and amendments
  • Attending to all security and insurance requirements
  • Secure storage of physical collateral
